Ariel

Ariel is a mermaid and one of the Walt Disney Princesses. She is the youngest daughter of King Triton and is most well known for her long, red, hair. Ariel's best friend is Flounder, a small fish that always has her back in times of need. She seems to be very rebellious but is very curious about the world above the sea. She eventually makes some life changing decisions that hlep her marry the man of her dreams.

Ariel makes her appearance in The Little Mermaid.
